Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided during registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter while using the app.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the Service, such as pages visited and features used.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface — this may result in a request being made to Google's servers when you visit the app. No personal expense data is involved in this request.

If we integrate any third-party services in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect what data, if any, is shared with them.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We will do our best to respond promptly.
